Data Breach & Security Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Draft for legal review

Security measures

We use industry-standard hosting (Vercel), database access controls (Supabase RLS), and HTTPS for all traffic. Admin functions require authenticated admin accounts.

If a breach occurs

  • We will investigate promptly and contain the incident.
  • Affected users and regulators will be notified where required by applicable law.
  • We will document the scope, cause, and remedial steps taken.
