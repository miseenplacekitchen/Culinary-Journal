This policy explains how The Culinary Journal uses cookies and similar technologies in your browser.

What we use

  • Essential storage — sign-in session, theme preference, and local grocery/meal-plan data stored in your browser.
  • Functional storage — recipe font size, notification read state, and similar settings that improve your experience.

What we do not do

We do not run third-party advertising cookies. We do not sell browsing data. Analytics, if enabled later, will be disclosed here before activation.

Your choices

You can clear site data through your browser settings. Clearing data will sign you out and reset local preferences.
