Credit & Preservation Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Draft for legal review

When a recipe source link stops working, we preserve the approved recipe content and attribution rather than deleting the work.

What we preserve

  • Contributor and named-chef credit on the live recipe page.
  • Original source URL reference where supplied, with a dead-link notice when verified unavailable.
  • Print Studio cards retain brand watermark and contributor attribution.

Contributor responsibility

Submitters must have the right to share the recipe. Imported or adapted recipes should name the original author or publication where known.

See also our Attribution Policy and DMCA procedure.
