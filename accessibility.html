Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2026 · Draft for review

The Culinary Journal is committed to making recipe browsing, planning, and hosting usable for as many people as possible, including older adults and users with visual or motor impairments.

Current measures

  • Keyboard-accessible navigation with visible focus indicators on interactive controls.
  • Skip-to-content link on pages that use the shared navigation.
  • Per-recipe text size controls (A through A+++) on the recipe page.
  • Semantic headings and form labels on account and submission flows.
  • Theme selection including high-contrast friendly dark themes.

Known limitations

Some visual themes and drag-and-drop planners are still being improved for screen-reader users. Printed cards and third-party embeds may not meet all contrast targets.

Feedback

If you encounter a barrier, email miseenplacekitchen.official@gmail.com with the page URL and a short description. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
