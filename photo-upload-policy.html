Photo Upload Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Draft for legal review

Only upload photos you own or have permission to use. Recipe hero images, profile avatars, and event photos must respect privacy and copyright.

Prohibited uploads

  • Copyrighted images without licence
  • Photos of minors without guardian consent
  • Explicit, violent, or unrelated imagery
  • Scanned pages from paid cookbooks or magazines

Our use

Approved recipe images may appear on public pages, browse cards, and print outputs with attribution to the contributor.
