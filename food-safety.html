Food Safety & Health Disclaimers

Last updated: June 2026 · Draft for legal review

Not medical advice. Recipes, nutrition estimates, preservation guidance, and baby-food notes are for general information only. They are not a substitute for professional medical, dietary, or food-safety advice.

Allergens & cross-contamination

Ingredient lists and allergen warnings are community- and database-assisted. They may be incomplete. Always verify labels, ask guests directly, and follow your own allergy protocols — especially for severe allergies.

Recipe outcomes

Cooking times, yields, and results vary with equipment, altitude, ingredient brands, and skill. Use your judgment and test doneness and temperature where food safety requires it.

Preservation & canning

Follow tested preservation methods and local food-safety authority guidance. Improper canning, fermenting, or curing can cause serious illness.

Baby & toddler food

Age-appropriate textures, allergen introduction, and choking hazards must be assessed by caregivers. Honey, salt, and certain foods are unsafe for infants per standard medical guidance.

Alcohol in cooking

Some alcohol may remain after cooking. Not all recipes are suitable for children, pregnancy, or recovery contexts.
