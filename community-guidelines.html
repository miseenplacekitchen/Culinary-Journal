The Culinary Journal

Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 2026 · Draft for legal review

The Culinary Journal is a respectful kitchen for home cooks, hosts, and contributors. These guidelines explain how we expect members to behave.

Be respectful

  • Treat other members, guests, and administrators with courtesy.
  • Do not harass, threaten, discriminate, or post hateful content.
  • Keep feedback constructive — especially in recipe reviews and event planning.

Share honestly

  • Submit only recipes and content you have the right to share.
  • Credit original authors and sources where required.
  • Do not impersonate another person or misrepresent your identity.

Keep the kitchen safe

  • Do not post dangerous instructions, illegal content, or spam.
  • Do not scrape, overload, or attempt to break site security.
  • Report concerns via the feedback tools or contact email below.

Enforcement

We may remove content, suspend accounts, or permanently deactivate accounts that breach these guidelines or our Terms of Use. Serious violations may be referred to appropriate authorities.
